Oppenheimer

Movie Review

Christopher Nolan as one of my favourite, if not favourite film directors. When I first heard that Oppenheimer was his next film, I was a bit skeptical. Oppenheimer is a drama/biography which is a genre Nolan has not tackled before. However, it turned out to be a great film that I enjoyed. I saw it with my family when it came out.

Bringing Down the House

Book Review

The book is written almost like a novel but it follows the five years of Kevin Lewis's life amongst his group of card counters and the successes they have in the casinos. The book is a great read especially because my topic for the I-search is gambling. It did not take long to read, and was very enjoayble. I reccomend this book to everyone.
